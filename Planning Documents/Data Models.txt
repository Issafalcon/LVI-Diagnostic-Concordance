Database Design
------------------------------------------------

1. LVIStudyUser (Custom IdentityUser)
	- FirstName (nvarchar50) - required
	- Last Name (nvarchar50) - required
	- Gender (nvarchar10) - Dropdown (male/female/Other)
	- Nationality (nvarchar 50) (optional?)
	- Work Place (Hospital) (nvarchar255) - (optional?)
	- Years of training (int32) required
	- Years as a Pathologist (int32) required
	- Subspecialty in Breast Pathology (boolean) - Dropdown field that is required
	- IsControlGroup (bool) (randomised and hidden from views)

2. Cases
	- CaseId (PK - Int) Indexed (cluistered by default) - One to many with UserCases
	- PatientAge (int32) - not null
	- TumourSize (decimal) - not null
	
3. Reports
	- ID (PK - Int) Indexed (clustered as default)
	- CaseId (FK) - Many to one join with Cases PK
	- UserName (FK - Many to one join with UserName PK)
	- Grade (int)
	- LVINumber (int)
	
4. ReportStatistics
	- LVI Present (bool)
	- ProbLVIPos50Plus (decimal)
	- ProbLVINeg50Plus (decimal)
	- BayesForAge (decimal)
	- ProbLVIPosSize (decimal)
	- ProbLVINegSize (decimal)
	- BayesForSize
	- ProbLVIPosGrade (decimal)
	- ProbLVINegGrade (decimal)
	- BayesForGrade
	- ProbLVIPosNumberOfLVI
	- ProbLVINegNumberOfLVI
	- BayesForNumberOfLVI
	
	- CumulativeBayesForSize (decimal)
	- CumulativeAverageBayesForSize
	- CumulativeCasesWithLVIPos (int)
	- BinomialDist
	- IsSubmitted (bool)
	
	- TheoreticalBinomialDist
